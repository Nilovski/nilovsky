CURIOSITY

I'm a Mechanical Engineering & Economics student at Vanderbilt University, pursuing a Minor in Nanoscience and Nanotechnology. A Cornelius Vanderbilt Merit Scholar, I bring both analytical rigor and creative problem-solving to every project.

My research spans from graphene CVD growth and tissue microfabrication at Vanderbilt's nanoscale labs, to ionized gas kinematics in distant galaxies at UC Santa Cruz, to exoplanet discovery at George Mason University.

I believe engineering and science are most powerful when they intersect disciplines — whether that's applying mesh optimization to aerospace, or combining economics with mechanical design to solve real-world challenges.
